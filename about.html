About Us

Happy Feet Swim School, located in Denver, Colorado, is dedicated to promoting health and well-being in the community through teaching swimming and lifesaving skills to children and adults. Swimming is full of health benefits and is an important life skill. That is why we thrive on creating a fun learning environment for all participants through our specially designed programs.

We offer a unique lesson curriculum following the American Red Cross guidelines. Programs are available to participants ages 4 months and up, ranging from toddler swim lessons to adult swim clubs. All Happy Feet instructors are Red Cross Certified Water Safety Instructors and undergo a comprehensive training period. We are committed to helping you become a better swimmer and are proud of the impact we are making on the community.
